Duo DUCROT – OPALIO
Duo Opalio - Ducrot are often praised for their sensibility and subtlety combined with impeccable togetherness and risk taking flair.
They began to play together as a duo after having co founded the Opalio Quintet. Playing chamber music together regularly since 2015, they won prizes at many national and international chamber music competitions such as ,Ysaye Competition (Belgium), Pinerolo Competition (Italy), Ibla Grand Prize (Italy), Orpheus Competition (Switzerland) and HSM Duo Competition in Basel (Switzerland).
They have performed concerts together throughout Europe in festivals and theatres such as, Engadin Festival, Mezzano Romantico, Adelboden Swiss Chamber Music Festival,Teatro Lorenzo da Ponte, Jeudis Musicales de Carcassonne.
The duo have received an international musical education with world class musicians such as, Raphael Oleg, Claudio Martinez Mehner, Jan Schultz, Anton Kernjak, Rainer Schmidt, Ferenc Rados. They are currently under the mentorship of Sir. Andràs Schiff and have been personally invited by him to the Beethoven festival in Bonn 2019. Chiara Opalio >>>

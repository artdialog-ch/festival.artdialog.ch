LES PASSIONS DU JUGLARFLAMENCO
Flamenco is full of emotion and intense expression. These qualities quickly engage spectators, regardless of where they are from or their knowledge about it.
Its music is filled with extreme feelings, affliction and joy. Rhythm is everywhere, dancers tapping their feet, clapping their hands, the percussive sound of the guitar. The passionate cry of the gypsy people fusioned with Andalusian folklore are the basis of this ancestral art.
Les passions du Flamenco is a conversation between five artists: Singer, guitarist and dancers, a dialogue of feeling, instict, and experience. It is a show that conveys the essence of this Spanish art form through its music and dance.
Each of the artists on stage will express their personality through this art form, respecting tradition, but also adding a current perception of this evolving art in a contrapunto with the rest of the ensemble.
This vision of flamenco as a total fusion of music, dance and feeling corresponds perfectly with the concept of the Festival ArtDialog. It because the concert of Les passions du Flamenco is a precious jewel in the artistic crown of the Festival.

Biografie
Two time Grammy award nominee Violinist Philippe Quint has emerged in recent years as one of the few young soloists to combine a remarkable degree of lyricism, poetry and impeccable virtuosity. His stage persona is evocative of a 19th century Romantic performer:

"With his lanky build and shoulder-length dark hair, he looked like a modern day Paganini - and he played like one, too."
Cincinnatti Enquirer.

He plays on a 1723 "Ex-Kiesewetter" Antonio Stradivari violin on loan to him from Clement and Karen Arrison through the generous efforts of the Stradivari Society.
